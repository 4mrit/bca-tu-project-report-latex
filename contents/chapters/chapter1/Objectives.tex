\section{Objectives}
The primary objectives of this project are:
\begin{itemize}
  \item To develop an e-commerce system that does not require technical expertise to operate.
  \item To provide an affordable platform that reduces the cost of creating an online shop.
  \item To create a structured system for managing products, orders, and customer information efficiently.
  \item To design a simple and user-friendly platform that is ready to use
\end{itemize}
