\chapter*{Abstract}

\para{
This report, titled \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/title}, documents the design, development, and deployment of a scalable e-commerce platform developed in partial fulfillment of the requirements for \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/degree} program. This project was undertaken at \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/institute/university} – \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/institute/faculty} of \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/institute/college}, located in \pgfkeysvalueof{/Project/institute/address}. 
}

\para{
The system leverages modern web technologies including .NET 8, Blazor Server, and PostgreSQL to provide a scalable, secure, and cost-effective e-commerce solution for small and medium enterprises. The platform enables shop owners to manage products, process orders, handle payments, and analyze sales through an intuitive SaaS-based interface. The project integrates industry best practices and iterative development methodologies to ensure robust functionality and user-friendly experience.
}

\para{
Through a structured development process encompassing thorough system analysis, modular design, and comprehensive testing—the deployed Sellion platform not only enhances digital presence for small businesses but also significantly improves operational efficiency through automated inventory management, secure payment processing, and real-time analytics. This work establishes a solid foundation for scalable e-commerce solutions and contributes valuable insights into the application of modern web technologies in the SME business domain.
}
